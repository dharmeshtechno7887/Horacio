Knight Dreams

Knight Dreams

At a young age, creator and craftsman, Zoltan David, became fascinated by detailed silver accents of a unique piece of inexpensive black jewelry. He envisioned that one day his hands would craft such an exquisite aesthetic, but with metals that would stand the test of time.

The Knight Dreams collection captures his unique designs, highlight Zoltan's personal advancements in metallurgy and old-world goldsmithing techniques - turning a vision into reality. Each hand-crafted piece is a sophisticated and captivating work of inspired imagination.

There are no black metals in nature.

For centuries metals have been blackened with patinas, oxides and platings. All of these treatments have a shelf life and erode in short order.

Zoltan has developed the "holy grail" in black metal, a highly wear and scratch resistant mirror-like finish; a midnight black that will hold its beauty indefinitely.

The Knight Dreams collection represents the tip of the spear in innovations in high jewelry design utilizing advanced material technology married with old world goldsmithing.
