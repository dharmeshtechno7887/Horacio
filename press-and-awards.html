Awards

  • 2014 - 1st Place Alternative Metals/Materials Saul Bell Design Award
  • 2012 - America's Coolest Jewelry Store Award - InStore Magazine
  • 2010 - Winner, Designers Award, International Pearl Design Competition, Cultured Association of America
  • 2009 - America's Coolest Jewelry Store - InStore Magazine
  • 2009 - April - "Best of Silver" National Jeweler Magazine Design Award
  • 2007 - AGTA Spectrum Award
  • 2007 - Tanzanite Foundation, 'Be Born To Tanzanite' Award
  • 2006 - Awarded US Patent # 7121118 for the 'Dangelier'
  • 2005 - Jewelry Designer of the Year - Contemporary Design Group
  • 2004 - AGTA Spectrum Award
  • 2003 - Awarded US Patent # 6594901 for his signature metal ornamentation technique.
  • 2001 - AGS Excellence in Overall Design Award
  • 2001 - AGS Excellence in Gold Design Award
  • 2001 - AGS Director's Choice Award
  • 1998 - Platinum Guild International, Platinum Passion Award
  • 1998 - AGTA Spectrum Award
  • 1996 - Finalist, Ponte d' Oro Competition, Italy
  • 1994* - Jewelers of America - New Designer of the Year, New York
  • 1994* - American Jewelry Design Council - New Design Talent, New York
  • 1992 - AGTA Spectrum Award
  • 1989 - AGTA Spectrum Award
  • 1979 - DeBeers Diamonds Today Award

* Zoltan is the first designer in the history of the JA Show to win both New Design Talent and New Designer of the Year.

Books

The Collector's Guide: Quintessentially Gems

Featured in "The Collector's Guide: Quintessentially Gems", an overview of historic and contemporary international jewelry artists, from the classics such as Bulgari, Cartier and Chopard, to today's innovators: Zoltan David, Wallace Chan, Francis Mertens, Cindy Chao and Theo Fennell, published by Quintessentially, the premier global luxury concierge service. Available on Amazon

Gemstone Masters: Major Works by Leading Jewelers
