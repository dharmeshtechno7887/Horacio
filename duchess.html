Duchess

Duchess Collection

Inspired by the opulance and architecture of his homeland - Budapest, Hungary - Zoltan David's Duchess Collection brings the essence of European-inspired delicate inlays and aristocratic motifs to life. These award-winning pieces have earned the recognition from the American Gem Society and a US patent for Zoltan's precious metal technique of inlaying to produce a unique three-dimensional raised design.

The iconic pieces of the Duchess collection are designed with a regal elegance that is both original and historic.
